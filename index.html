Our project

Apple is an American corporation, a world-famous manufacturer of personal and tablet computers, audio players, smartphones, and software. At the moment, the price of this product is higher than all others on the market of Kazakhstan. This is why we decided to create a site to sell Apple products at the lowest prices. TEMIRTAS is an official partner of Apple in Kazakhstan, the leading reseller of Apple goods in Kazakhstan. At Apples, not only original products and accessories at pleasant prices, but also a professional service center.
